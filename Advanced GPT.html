ADVANCED GPT

Rebranding for Advanced GPT – At Wyte Studio, we had the privilege of leading the rebranding efforts for Advanced GPT, a cutting-edge AI company. Our mission was to refresh their brand identity to align with their innovative technology and forward-thinking approach.

We revamped the logo with a modern, tech-forward design that reflects Advanced GPT’s expertise in artificial intelligence. The new visual identity incorporates sleek typography, bold colors, and a clean, futuristic look to convey their commitment to innovation. Along with the logo, we crafted a comprehensive brand identity, including updated color schemes, typography, and brand messaging. The rebranding ensures consistency across all platforms, giving Advanced GPT a stronger presence in the tech industry.
