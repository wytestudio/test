DCH - BUSINESS SETUP

Branding & Logo Design for Dubai Corporative Hub – At Wyte Studio, we had the opportunity to create the branding and logo for DCH - Dubai Corporative Hub, a business setup company in Dubai. Our goal was to craft a modern and professional identity that aligns with their mission to simplify business establishment in the UAE.

The logo features sleek typography, deep blues, and metallic accents to symbolize trust and growth. The hub icon emphasizes connectivity, while the stylized "D" represents Dubai and dynamic progress. We developed a cohesive brand identity, ensuring consistency across all platforms, from business cards to digital assets. The result is a memorable and professional brand that reflects DCH’s expertise in helping businesses thrive in Dubai.
